Bubbles Aquarium

Concept

Inspired by the quiet motion of water, this project recreates an abstract aquarium using HTML5 canvas animation. Bubbles drift upward at different speeds, gently responding to the viewer’s presence through hover-based motion and color shifts.

The page is designed with an off-center layout, pairing written content on the left with the animated bubble field on the right. This contrast creates visual interest while maintaining unity through color, spacing, and rhythm.

Design Decisions

The color palette centers around deep blue tones with bright cyan and soft mint accents to evoke a gentle underwater atmosphere. Using only a few intentional colors keeps the design cohesive while allowing the bubbles to feel lively and expressive.

To support readability, the layout relies on alignment and spacing to group related content. A clear typographic hierarchy ranging from bold headings to smaller supporting text guides the viewer through the page in an intuitive way.

Interaction

When the cursor enters the bubble field, individual bubbles respond with a soft glow and gentle expansion, creating the feeling of interaction within the underwater space. This rollover effect highlights how motion graphics can guide a viewer’s focus while maintaining a calm visual rhythm.
