About the Movie

Ghost in the Shell (1995) is a landmark in cyberpunk cinema, directed by Mamoru Oshii. Set in a dystopian future, it explores themes of identity, artificial intelligence, and the nature of humanity.

Cast & Crew

  • Director: Mamoru Oshii
  • Major Motoko Kusanagi (Voice): Atsuko Tanaka
  • Batou (Voice): Akio Ōtsuka
  • Music: Kenji Kawai
