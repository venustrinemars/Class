About IVE

IVE (아이브) is a 6-member K-pop girl group under Starship Entertainment, debuting December 1, 2021. Their name, “I HAVE,” reflects the members’ confidence and pride in showcasing everything they bring to the stage.

With chart-topping singles, viral performances, and a global fandom known as “DIVE,” IVE deliver bold vocals, striking visuals, and an electrifying pop-sound that celebrates ambition and style.
