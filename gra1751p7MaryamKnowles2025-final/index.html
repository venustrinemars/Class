About Mayelewo

Mayelewo is a riverside ramen bar glowing with neon color, steam, and sound. We serve bold broths, bright toppings, and playful small bites under city lights reflected in the water.

Ramen & Bites Highlights

River Glow Tonkotsu

A rich, luminous pork broth simmered for hours until silky and full-bodied. Served with tender chashu pork, springy noodles, scallions, earthy mushrooms, and a perfectly molten ajitama egg.

Midnight Miso Neon

A deep, dark, and boldly savory miso ramen with caramelized undertones inspired by late-night city energy.Featuring roasted chashu pork, wilted spinach, scallions, toasted sesame, rich miso paste, and a soft-boiled egg resting in a glossy midnight broth.

Firecracker Gyoza

Crispy, golden pan-seared dumplings bursting with a spicy, flavorful filling. Served with a zesty sesame-soy dipping sauce and topped with scallions and chili threads for an extra kick.
